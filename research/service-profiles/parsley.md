## Summary

**Parsley Health is primarily a functional medicine practice, not a membership health testing service.** It has two distinct products relevant to this comparison:

### Longevity Labs ($350/yr) — The Comparable Product
- ~80-100 biomarkers (claimed), 1 draw/yr at Quest
- 13 panel categories including CBC, metabolic, lipid, thyroid, hormones, inflammation, liver, kidney, electrolytes, autoimmune, adrenal, nutrients, environmental toxins
- Clinician-reviewed dashboard with optional $200 clinical review visit
- Biological age / "Functional Age" calculation
- **Strengths:** Affordable ($350), broad panel categories including environmental toxins and autoimmune, clinician review available, prescribing possible via Complete Care upsell
- **Weaknesses:** No published biomarker list (major transparency gap), no wearable integration, no data export, no mobile app, originally female-focused (male panel uncertain), only 1 draw/yr, 10-14 day turnaround, relatively new product (Nov 2025 relaunch)

### Complete Care ($2,750/yr) — Not Directly Comparable
- Functional medicine membership with physician access, coaching, messaging
- Labs ordered a-la-carte by physicians — not a standardized panel
- Lab costs are EXTRA (billed through insurance or out-of-pocket)
- Strong for people seeking ongoing functional medicine care, but not a "health testing service" in the comparison category

> **Key Question:** Does Fountain Life's blood testing component ALONE qualify (50+ directly measured biomarkers), or is it bundled with imaging/other services at a much higher price point?
>
> **Answer:** Fountain Life's blood panel claims 100+ biomarkers, which would qualify on biomarker count alone. HOWEVER, the blood panel is NOT available as a standalone purchase — it is bundled into comprehensive longevity memberships starting at $10,500/yr (CORE) that include full-body MRI, cardiac CT, genetic sequencing, and many other diagnostics. There is no blood-testing-only tier. This makes Fountain Life fundamentally a different category than services like Function Health ($365/yr) or Superpower ($199/yr) that offer blood testing as their core standalone product.

## Summary

### What Fountain Life IS
Fountain Life is a **premium, center-based longevity diagnostics and therapeutics platform** backed by Tony Robbins and Peter Diamandis. It combines comprehensive blood testing (100+ claimed markers) with advanced imaging (full-body MRI, cardiac CT), genetic sequencing, functional assessments (VO2 max, cognitive testing), and regenerative therapeutics (stem cells, plasma exchange, HRT). It is a fundamentally different product category than blood-testing-only services.

### What Fountain Life is NOT
It is NOT a standalone blood testing membership comparable to Function Health ($365/yr), Superpower ($199/yr), or similar services. The blood panel is one component of a comprehensive diagnostic suite.

### for a longevity-focused member

**Pros:**
- Available in the US (Dallas center open; Houston coming soon)
- Comprehensive blood panel (100+ markers) with advanced cardiovascular markers (ApoB, Lp(a), MPO, Lp-PLA2)
- Includes many high-value diagnostics beyond blood: full-body MRI, cardiac CT, whole genome sequencing, DEXA, VO2 max
- Garmin integration via app
- AI interpretation (Zori) + physician review
- Epigenetic biological age
- Multi-cancer early detection (Galleri) available

**Cons:**
- **Price:** $10,500/yr minimum (29x the cost of Function Health for blood testing alone)
- **No standalone blood option:** Cannot isolate the blood panel
- **No public biomarker list:** Transparency concern — cannot verify exactly what's tested
- **Center-based only:** Must travel to a Fountain Life center (no at-home draws, no Quest/Labcorp)
- **Limited locations:** Only 4 centers currently open
- **Data export unclear:** No confirmed CSV/PDF export capability
- **1 draw/yr at CORE tier:** Insufficient for ongoing monitoring at the lowest price point
- **Experimental therapies:** Some offered treatments (stem cells, exosomes) are not FDA-approved
- **Hidden costs:** Follow-up care, travel, recommended devices can add $2,000+ to annual cost

### Disqualification Assessment

| Criterion | Status |

| Provides numerical test results | YES (via app) |
| 50+ directly measured biomarkers | LIKELY YES (~75-90 confirmed, claims 100+) |
| Available in the US | YES (Dallas open) |

**Recommendation:** Fountain Life should be included in the comparison matrix as a reference point for "premium longevity platform" category, but it is NOT a direct competitor to standalone blood testing services. for a longevity-focused member seeking cost-effective comprehensive blood monitoring with raw data access, Fountain Life is dramatically overpriced for the blood testing component alone. However, if the user is interested in the FULL diagnostic suite (MRI, CT, genetic, functional + blood), it represents a comprehensive option at the premium end.

**Cost per directly measured blood biomarker (estimated):** $10,500 / ~85 markers = ~$124/marker (CORE) vs. Function Health at $365 / ~110 markers = ~$3.32/marker. This is a 37x cost difference for blood testing alone, though Fountain Life includes substantial additional diagnostics that justify some of the premium.

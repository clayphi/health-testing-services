## A. Pricing & Membership

### Tier Overview

Wild Health is a **physician-led concierge precision medicine** service — NOT a pure biomarker testing service like Function Health or Superpower. It bundles genetics, bloodwork, physician visits, and health coaching into a medical membership. This fundamentally changes the value proposition and comparison basis.

## Summary

**Wild Health is fundamentally a different category of service** compared to Function Health, Superpower, Hundred Health, or Mito Health. It is a **physician-led concierge medicine membership** that happens to include bloodwork, not a biomarker testing service that provides results and data.

### Key Differentiators (Positive)
- Real physician relationship with quarterly visits (not just AI interpretation)
- Genetic analysis (700,000+ SNPs) integrated with blood panel interpretation
- Biological age testing (TruDiagnostic) included 2x/year
- Personalized protocols for nutrition, exercise, supplements
- Health coaching unlimited
- Prescribing ability (non-DEA scheduled)
- Available in all 50 states including the US

### Key Weaknesses for Our Use Case
- **Price:** $4,345/yr (Standard) — 10-12x the cost of Function Health ($365) or Superpower ($199-388) for FEWER biomarkers in the base panel
- **Biomarker coverage:** Base panel covers only ~31-42% of our 45-test target — worst among all compared services
- **Many key markers are add-ons** at unknown additional cost (Free T3/T4, SHBG, DHEA-S, Cortisol, IGF-1, Lp(a), NMR, Lp-PLA2, Zinc, Copper)
- **No Garmin integration** — dealbreaker for longevity-focused profile
- **Questionable data access** — user complaints about inability to get raw data
- **No CSV/API export** confirmed
- **Operational issues** — billing complaints, delayed results, communication problems
- **Cannot prescribe testosterone** or other DEA-scheduled medications (limits utility as concierge medicine)

### Disqualification Assessment

| Criterion | Status |
|---|---|
| Provides numerical test results | YES (via patient portal) |
| 50+ directly measured biomarkers | BORDERLINE — claims 65-68 but verifiable base panel is ~30-35 measured. Add-ons may push past 50 but at additional unknown cost. |
| Available in the US | YES |

**RECOMMENDATION:** Wild Health should NOT be disqualified but scores very poorly for our specific use case. It is optimized for someone who wants a physician-guided wellness journey with genetics emphasis, NOT for someone who wants comprehensive biomarker data, raw data access, and wearable integration at reasonable cost. At $4,345/yr for ~31-42% target panel coverage vs. $365/yr for ~73-78% coverage (Function Health), the value proposition does not hold for data-focused health optimization.

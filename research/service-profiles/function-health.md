## Section A: Pricing & Membership

### A1. Price Per Year
- **$365/year** (reduced from $499 in November 2025) [Multi-source verified]
- Equivalent to ~$30.42/month or ~$1/day
- The $365 price is described as "first-year membership" in fine print, though current messaging indicates renewals are also $365 [Single-source only]

### A2. Tier Options
- **One tier only.** No basic/premium/elite split. [Multi-source verified]
- All members get identical access to base testing, dashboard, clinician review, and add-on pricing.

### A3. What the Membership Includes
- **Annual Test (Draw 1):** 100+ lab tests across all major organ systems [Multi-source verified]
- **Mid-Year Test (Draw 2):** 60+ lab tests (core subset for trend tracking), 3-6 months after Draw 1 [Multi-source verified]
- Clinician review of every result with written notes [Multi-source verified]
- Personalized action plan with food and supplement recommendations (marked "ALPHA") [Multi-source verified]
- Biological age calculation (2x/year) [Multi-source verified]
- Dashboard with color-coded results, trend tracking, and downloadable PDF [Multi-source verified]
- Private AI Chat (launched Nov 2025) [Multi-source verified]
- Health-record upload capability [Single-source only]
- "Protocols" feature for actionable guidance [Multi-source verified]
- 9 health guides [Single-source only]
- Access to member-only add-on test pricing [Multi-source verified]

### A4. Draws Per Year Included
- **2 draws included** (Annual Test + Mid-Year Test) [Multi-source verified]
- Annual Test may require 2 appointments within ~10 days in some cases (different vials) [Multi-source verified]
- Arizona residents require 2 visits per draw due to state processing rules [Single-source only]
- Most members complete each draw in a single 15-minute visit (~10 vials, ~35mL, 3-4 min actual draw time) [Multi-source verified]

### A5. Cost of Additional Draws
- On-demand re-testing of any biomarker available at "member-only cost" [Multi-source verified]
- Specific re-test pricing is NOT publicly listed — shown only in the member dashboard [Multi-source verified]
- No publicly confirmed price for individual re-tests [Unverified]

### A6. At-Home Draw Option and Cost
- **No at-home draw option offered by Function Health.** All draws are at Quest Diagnostics Patient Service Centers. [Multi-source verified]
- Third-party mobile phlebotomy services ($75-150/visit) could theoretically be used, but Function does not offer or coordinate this. [Single-source only]

### A7. Lab Draw Fees
- Lab draw fees are **generally included** in the membership price [Multi-source verified]
- **Exception:** In some states, Quest Diagnostics may charge a separate lab/draw fee. One reviewer (dannb.org, pre-price-drop era) reported paying "just over $200" per Quest visit on top of membership. [Single-source only]
- **New York and New Jersey:** Testing "can cost more due to state regulations" — Function walks members through details during scheduling [Multi-source verified]
- Most members in most states report no additional lab fees [Multi-source verified]

### A8. HSA/FSA Eligibility
- **Yes, HSA/FSA eligible** [Multi-source verified]
- Members receive itemized receipts via email within 1 business day of scheduling labs [Single-source only]
- Caveat: "Reimbursement is not guaranteed" — depends on specific plan terms [Multi-source verified]
- Effective cost after HSA/FSA tax savings: ~$235-275/year depending on tax bracket [Single-source only]

### A9. Cancellation/Refund Policy
- **48-hour refund window:** Cancel within 48 hours of initial purchase (if no lab testing received) for full refund. Policy effective Sept 1, 2025. [Multi-source verified]
- **Add-on refunds:** Cancel within 48 hours of add-on purchase (if no lab testing received) for full refund [Single-source only]
- **No refunds** for payments outside the 48-hour window [Multi-source verified]
- **No pause option** available [Multi-source verified]
- Cancel anytime and rejoin later [Multi-source verified]
- Cancellation process reported as buried and difficult to find by some users [Multi-source verified]

### A10. Hidden Costs Reported by Users
- **Quest lab fees in some states** (reported $200+ in one case, pre-price-drop era) [Single-source only]
- **NY/NJ surcharges** due to state lab regulations [Multi-source verified]
- **Add-on tests** can easily push total to $1,000+ (Galleri $899, Lyme $549, etc.) [Multi-source verified]
- **Auto-renewal:** Some App Store reviewers report unexpected auto-renewal charges [Multi-source verified]
- **No hidden signup fees** beyond annual membership [Multi-source verified]

### A11. Price Per Month Equivalent
- **$30.42/month** ($365 / 12) [Multi-source verified]
- Chase Sapphire Reserve cardholders reportedly get 50% statement credit, effectively $182.50/year (~$15.21/month) [Single-source only]
- With HSA/FSA tax savings: ~$19.58-22.92/month effective cost [Single-source only]

## Section C: Features & Amenities

### C1. Dashboard / App Quality
- **Web app:** Yes — full-featured dashboard accessible via browser [Multi-source verified]
- **iOS app:** Yes — rated 4.9/5 with 18,496 ratings on App Store. Current version 1.21.0 (updated April 2, 2026). Requires iOS 15.5+. [Multi-source verified]
- **Android app:** Yes — rated 3.7/5 with 44 ratings on Google Play. Updated April 2, 2026. [Multi-source verified]
- **UI quality:** Universally praised as clean, intuitive, color-coded (green = in range, yellow/red = out of range). Each biomarker shows your value vs optimal range, "Why it matters?" explanation, potential reasons for out-of-range results, and trend tracking over time. [Multi-source verified]
- **Criticisms:** Some App Store reviewers found UX "clunky and counter-intuitive" with "repetitive steps." [Multi-source verified]

### C2. Results Interpretation
- **Clinician review:** Licensed clinicians authorize tests and review every result with written summary (~400-500 words). Notes address concerning biomarkers with category-specific guidance referencing intake survey responses. [Multi-source verified]
- **AI Chat:** "Private AI Chat" launched Nov 2025, built in partnership with top clinicians. Allows questions about results, pattern identification, and focus recommendations. [Multi-source verified]
- **"Chat with a Doctor" feature:** Reported in beta testing as of April 2026. [Single-source only — healnourishgrow.com]
- **1:1 clinician consultation:** NOT available. No phone call, no video, no live Q&A. [Multi-source verified]
- **Critical results:** Trigger an immediate phone call from a healthcare provider. [Single-source only]
- **Quality of clinician notes:** Widely reported as generic and likely AI-generated/AI-assisted. Multiple Reddit users and blog reviewers express disappointment. [Multi-source verified]

### C3. Wearable Integration
- **Apple Health:** Yes — connect Apple Health to see how activity, sleep, and daily habits affect lab results. [Multi-source verified]
- **Garmin:** NOT specifically mentioned in any Function Health documentation or app description. [Multi-source verified]
- **Workaround for Garmin:** Garmin Connect can sync to Apple Health (on iOS), which Function can then read. This is an indirect, user-configured bridge — not a native Function + Garmin integration. [Single-source only]
- **Other wearables:** The app mentions "other health devices and wearables" generically but does not list specific supported devices beyond Apple Health. [Single-source only]

### C4. Medical Record Import
- **Health-record upload capability** is mentioned in Function's feature list [Single-source only]
- Unclear whether this is manual PDF upload or automated import from health systems (like Hundred Health's 300+ system integration) [Unverified]
- No confirmed integration with Epic MyChart, Cerner, or other EHR systems [Unverified]

### C5. Health Protocol / Action Plan
- **"Your Action Plan" feature:** Marked "ALPHA" (preliminary/beta). Includes personalized top-5 foods to enjoy/avoid, extended food lists, and supplement suggestions based on out-of-range results. [Multi-source verified]
- **"Protocols" feature:** Actionable guidance for improving specific biomarkers. [Multi-source verified]
- **9 health guides** for "living 100 healthy years" [Single-source only]

### C6. Supplement Recommendations
- **Included in the action plan** — supplement suggestions based on your specific out-of-range results [Multi-source verified]
- **NOT an upsell/affiliate model** — Function does not sell supplements or take affiliate commissions (unlike Superpower which has $400+/mo AI supplement recommendations) [Multi-source verified]
- Quality of recommendations reported as "generic and not tailored" by some users [Multi-source verified]

### C7. Biological Age Calculation
- **Methodology:** Proprietary algorithm using ~130 blood biomarkers across metabolic, cardiovascular, liver, kidney, hormone, nutrient, and inflammatory panels. NOT DNA methylation-based. [Multi-source verified]
- **Frequency:** Calculated 2x/year (after each draw) [Multi-source verified]
- **Reported results:** One 36-year-old got 24.2; another reviewer got 16.5 [Multi-source verified]
- **Limitation:** No standardized biological age methodology exists. Different platforms give different results — variation of nearly 8 years reported across 3 services. [Multi-source verified]

### C8. Historical Trend Tracking
- **Yes** — dashboard shows trend tracking over time for returning members [Multi-source verified]
- Color-coded visualizations with each biomarker plotted over multiple draws [Multi-source verified]
- Category summaries at bottom of home screen [Single-source only]

### C9. PDF/CSV Data Export
- **PDF download:** Yes — downloadable PDF to share with your doctor [Multi-source verified]
- **CSV/raw data export:** NOT natively available in the Function app or dashboard [Multi-source verified]
- **Workaround:** A third-party Chrome extension (github.com/Greenband1/biomarker_chrome_extension) can extract biomarker data from Function Health into CSV, JSON, or clipboard-friendly table formats. Includes Quest test codes for external mapping. All processing is local/browser-side. [Single-source only]
- **No official API** for data export or integration [Multi-source verified]

### C10. API Access / Integration with Other Health Apps
- **No public API** [Multi-source verified]
- **Apple Health integration** for importing wearable data INTO Function (not exporting lab data OUT) [Multi-source verified]
- **No integration** with Apple Health for lab results, Google Health Connect, or other health platforms [Unverified]
- **Empirical Health** has documented a process for exporting Function Health results into their platform [Single-source only]

### C11. Physician Access
- **No live physician access.** Function is explicitly "not a medical provider." [Multi-source verified]
- **No telehealth** consultations [Multi-source verified]
- **No prescribing ability** [Multi-source verified]
- **Clinician notes only** — written summaries, no interactive Q&A [Multi-source verified]
- **"Chat with a Doctor" beta** reportedly in development as of April 2026 [Single-source only]

### C12. Community Features
- **No community forums or member events** documented [Unverified]
- **r/Function_Health subreddit** exists as an unofficial community (~active, multiple threads) [Multi-source verified]
- **No Discord, Slack, or other community platform** [Unverified]

### D4. Fasting Requirements
- **8-hour fast minimum** (no food or drink except water) [Multi-source verified]
- **72 hours before:** Stop all supplements [Multi-source verified]
- **48 hours before:** Avoid seafood (mercury testing accuracy) [Multi-source verified]
- **Morning of:** Drink 1+ liters of water; avoid exercise [Multi-source verified]
- Morning testing preferred for hormone accuracy (cortisol is diurnal) [Multi-source verified]

2. Log into Function dashboard [Multi-source verified]
3. Schedule lab visit at an approved Quest Diagnostics location [Multi-source verified]
4. Choose date/time [Multi-source verified]

## Summary

1. **Best-in-class biomarker breadth for the price:** At $365/year for 2 draws with ~92 directly measured markers (including full NMR LipoProfile, ApoB, Lp(a), full autoimmunity screen, heavy metals, omega-3/6 panel, and pancreatic enzymes), Function Health offers the strongest biomarker-per-dollar ratio among membership health testing services. [Multi-source verified]

2. **Interpretation is the weak link:** The clinician review is widely reported as generic/AI-generated, and there is no live physician interaction. The new AI Chat and rumored "Chat with a Doctor" beta may address this over time, but as of April 2026, users needing clinical guidance must bring results to their own physician. [Multi-source verified]

3. **No native Garmin integration:** for a longevity-focused member, Function Health does NOT directly integrate with Garmin. Apple Health integration exists, and Garmin can sync to Apple Health as a workaround on iOS. Multiple services now claim Garmin integration: Hundred Health ($499, UNVERIFIED), Superpower (added v1.0.6, March 2026), Fountain Life (confirmed via Athletech News), and InsideTracker (confirmed native Garmin support). Depth and quality of each integration are unverified for all. [Multi-source verified -- superpower.com changelog, athletechnews.com, insidetracker.com]

4. **73-78% coverage of target 45-test panel:** Function covers 33/45 tests directly, 2 indirectly (B12 via MMA, Omega-3 via serum), and 2 via add-ons. The 8 missing tests (C-Peptide, Copper, Ceruloplasmin, ESR, Fibrinogen, LP-PLA2, Oxidized LDL, Cystatin C) require gap-fill ordering through Marek, Ulta, or similar DTC services at an estimated $250-465 additional cost. [Estimated -- gap-fill pricing from marekhealth.com and ultalabtests.com; specific product page URLs not cited]

5. **Strong institutional backing but early-stage clinical experience:** Founded 2021, $354M+ raised at $2.5B valuation, 100K+ members, distinguished advisory board (Harvard, Cleveland Clinic, Stanford, MSK). However, the service is effectively a well-curated lab ordering platform on top of Quest Diagnostics — not a clinical service. The Trustpilot rating (4.3/5, 997 reviews) and iOS rating (4.9/5, 18.5K ratings) indicate solid but not universal satisfaction. [Multi-source verified]

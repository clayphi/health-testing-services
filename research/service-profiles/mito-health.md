## Summary

**Mito Health** is a young (founded 2023), YC-backed startup offering comprehensive blood + urine testing at competitive pricing. Its Core panel ($349) includes several markers rare among competitors — notably **RBC Magnesium, B12, Folate, ESR, Free T3, Amylase/Lipase, and a full 21-marker urinalysis** — giving it strong breadth for the price. The Pro tier ($549) adds valuable longevity markers (Cystatin C, IGF-1, cholesterol particles, Omega-3/6, heavy metals) that would cost $89-99 each as add-ons.

**Strengths for longevity-focused profile:**
- Best-in-class marker breadth at Core price ($349) — includes RBC Mg, B12, Folate, ESR, full iron panel, PSA Total+Free
- Pro tier ($549) adds Cystatin C and IGF-1 that most competitors miss entirely
- 1:1 clinician consultation + AI chatbot included
- All 50 states claimed (including TX)
- HSA/FSA eligible

**Weaknesses for longevity-focused profile:**
- Only 1 draw/year (vs Function Health's 2, Hundred Health's 2)
- No wearable integration (no Garmin sync)
- No dedicated mobile app
- Missing from 45-panel: C-Peptide, TPO Ab, TgAb, Zinc, Selenium, Copper, Ceruloplasmin, Fibrinogen, LP-PLA2, Oxidized LDL
- Very young company with small team — longevity/stability risk
- No CSV/raw data export confirmed
- Lab network details inconsistent across sources

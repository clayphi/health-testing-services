### 4b. 2-draw model — CORROBORATED as still in place (incl. reduced Draw 2)
- Multiple sources confirm the membership still includes **two draws/year: a comprehensive baseline (160+ markers framed historically; "100+" on current site) + a focused follow-up panel of 50+ biomarkers at ~100 days** to measure change. The app's "retest" flow lets members "select key biomarkers" for the progress draw.
  - Source: outliyr.com/best-blood-biomarker-testing-services (2026); App Store retest description; healnourishgrow.com (review dated 2026-03-16, body text "160+ lab tests across two draws").
- So **Draw 2 is still a reduced ~50+ marker progress panel** (answers open Q on Draw 2 scope). Note current homepage marketing rounds the headline count down to "100+ lab tests."
- **M3 draw-count reconciliation (fix cycle 1):** the soft phrasings across files are consistent ranges, not contradictions. web-03 F10 (App Store + company-response sourced) gives the more precise reviewer figures: **Draw 1 ≈ 110 markers; Draw 2 = a fixed standardized panel of ≈ 55 markers**, with expanded follow-up testing costing ~$300 extra. Read the "50+" / "100+/160+" figures here as the rounded marketing/historical ranges and the "~110 / ~55" figures in web-03 as the observed per-draw counts. The "160+ across two draws" historical claim = Draw 1 (~110) + Draw 2 (~55) summed (same markers counted twice). The 2-draw model itself is corroborated by 3 third-party sources but is **not stated in plain text on the current hundred.com homepage/FAQ** (accordion-collapsed) → labeled corroborated-not-primary.
- **Freshness:** corroborated across 3 third-party sources retrieved 2026-06; not stated on current hundred.com homepage/FAQ in plain text (accordion-collapsed), so labeled corroborated-not-primary.

## Key Findings

- **PRIMARY: Current price is $199/year ($0.55/day), billed annually** — confirmed on the live hundred.com homepage (retrieved 2026-06-08). This is a **~$300 (~60%) drop** from the $499 baseline, matching the user's report that Hundred "dropped their prices a ton." The old $499 ($1.37/day) figure now appears only as an anchor/comparison.
- **Still a single membership tier**; no new tier structure detected.
- **Still 2 draws/year**, and **Draw 2 is still a reduced ~50+ marker progress panel** at ~100 days (corroborated by 3 third-party sources; not in plain text on current site).
- **Add-on prices unchanged**: DUTCH $300, DNA methylation $180; Galleri/Prenuvo/CGM still pre-launch ("Coming Q1 2026") with no published price.
- **Refund policy unchanged** (effective 12/01/2024): 2-day full-refund window, partial after, Technology Fee retained.
- **HSA/FSA: still yes. NY/NJ surcharges: still apply, amount still unpublished.**
- **Secondary changes**: lab network added **LabCorp** (now Quest + BioReference + LabCorp); EHR import grew **300+ → 450+** systems; homepage marketing count shifted "160+" → "100+" lab tests.
- **Still Unverified (carry to Open Questions):** extra/3rd-draw cost (#18), exact at-home phlebotomy fee (#19, industry $75–$150), exact NY/NJ surcharge amount, Galleri/Prenuvo/CGM prices (#51), and whether the $50 referral still applies at the new $199 price. FAQ answers for several of these are JS-accordion-collapsed and not machine-fetchable; would require direct support contact to confirm exact dollar amounts.
